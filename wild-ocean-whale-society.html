Wild Ocean Whale Society

Wild Ocean Whale Society (WOWs) is a registered B.C. charity incorporated in 2013 which evolved from a blog under the Whales and Dolphins BC banner started in 2010 by our founder, Susan MacKay to raise public awareness, respect and enthusiasm for the protection and welfare of our coastal wildlife and marine ecosystems through information and education. (quoted from the Society website)

Biomonitoring Programs: Yes, logging of whale sightings.

Education Programs: Yes; information brochures, community presentations and appearances at local events, and other efforts.

Biomonitoring and Community Science Volunteer Opportunities: Yes, by reporting whale sightings.

Website: https://wildoceanwhale.org/
Location: Powell River
Contact: https://wildoceanwhale.org/contact-us
