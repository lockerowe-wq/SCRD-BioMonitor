Hotel Lake Advisory Association

Biomonitoring: Yes. Completes extensive water quality monitoring in conjunction with the Ministry of Environment and UBC. Records activities of fish, birds, aquatic invertebrates and mammals on the lake.

Education: No. However, their website is in large part devoted to education about Hotel Lake and related topics.

Biomonitoring and Community Science Volunteer Opportunities: Yes. The Association relies on local volunteers. Contact the Association to learn about opportunities.
