Hotel Lake Advisory Association

Biomonitoring Programs: Yes. Records activities of fish, birds, aquatic invertebrates and mammals on the lake. Completes extensive water quality monitoring in conjunction with the Ministry of Environment and UBC.

Education Programs: No. However, their website is in large part devoted to education about Hotel Lake and related topics.

Biomonitoring and Community Science Volunteer Opportunities: No current opportunities.

Website: https://www.hotellakeadvisory.com
Location: Garden Bay
