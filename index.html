Organizations Involved

  • The Loon Foundation: Dedicated to protecting and restoring wetland habitats across the Sunshine Coast.
  • Sargeant Bay Society: Focused on environmental stewardship and conservation of local ecosystems.
  • Sunshine Coast Streamkeepers Society: Engages in monitoring and rehabilitating local stream health.

Additional Opportunities

  • BC Nature: A non-profit organization that supports conservation efforts.
  • eBird: A citizen science project that allows people to report bird sightings.
  • iNaturalist: An online platform for sharing biodiversity research and observations.
