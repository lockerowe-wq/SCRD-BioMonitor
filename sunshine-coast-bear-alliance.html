Sunshine Coast Bear Alliance

Our mission "Is to end negative human-bear encounters on our Sunshine Coast by fostering a pragmatic understanding and appreciation of our coast's bears that will promote a safe and respectful coexistence between our bears, residents and visitors to the community." (Quoted from the Alliance website)

Biomonitoring Programs: No known current programs.

Education Programs: Yes; information brochures, community presentations and appearances at local farmers markets, and other efforts.

Biomonitoring and Community Science Volunteer Opportunities: No

Website: https://www.scbearalliance.com/
Location: Sechelt
Contact: scbearalliance@gmail.com
