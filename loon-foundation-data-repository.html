The Loon Foundation

Data Repository

Forage fish: This program is part of the Coastal Forage Fish Network. These data are shared with the shíshálh and Tla'amin Nations and are deposited with the Pacific Salmon Foundation and can be accessed at https://soggy2.zoology.ubc.ca/geonetwork/srv/eng/catalog.search#/metadata/904a8e86-7992-424a-9b68-40906852f4e9

Salmon Escapement Monitoring: Data shared with Fisheries and Oceans Canada. These data appear in the periodic South Coast Salmon Strait of Georgia Stock Assessment.

Juvenile Salmon Monitoring: These data are shared with Fisheries and Oceans Canada. More information and some data can be found at https://www.loonfoundation.org/cwmp in their Biomonitoring Reports.

Seagrass Monitoring: Data not currently shared. More information and some data can be found at https://www.loonfoundation.org/cwmp in their Biomonitoring Reports.

Intertidal Macroinvertebrates and Seaweeds Monitoring: Data not currently shared. More information and some data can be found at https://www.loonfoundation.org/cwmp in their Biomonitoring Reports.

Pinniped monitoring: Data not currently shared. More information and some data can be found at https://www.loonfoundation.org/cwmp in their Biomonitoring Reports.

Coastal Waterbirds: Data shared with Bird Studies Canada's Nature Counts and eBird. (https://ebird.org/profile/MjYzNTYwNQ/CA-BC-SC)

Loon Monitoring: Data shared with Bird Studies Canada Loon Lakes Monitoring Program.

Larval Crab Monitoring: Data shared with the Hakai Institute's Sentinels of Change program and the Pacific Northwest Crab Research Group.

European Green Crab: Data shared with the shíshálh and Tla'amin Nations and Fisheries and Oceans Canada.
