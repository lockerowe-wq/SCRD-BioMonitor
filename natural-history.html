Natural History of the Sunshine Coast

Local Natural History Guides

Projects of the non-profits featured in this site include natural history guides aimed at helping the public get to know the biodiversity around them.

Birds
Bird Checklist of the Sunshine Coast
Tony Greenfield, Rand Rudland & John Hodges
A project of the Sunshine Coast Natural History Society. Free for SCNHS members; available for a nominal fee for non-members.
Insects
Butterflies of the Sunshine Coast
Tony Greenfield and Rand Rudland
A project of the Sunshine Coast Natural History and Sargeant Bay Societies. Available in local bookstores.
Insects
Dragonflies of the Sunshine Coast
Rand Rudland
A project of the Sunshine Coast Natural History and Sargeant Bay Societies. Available in local bookstores.
