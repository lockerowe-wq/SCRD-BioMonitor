Natural History of the Sunshine Coast

Projects of the non-profits featured in this page include natural history guides aimed at helping the public get to know the biodiversity around them. These include:

  • Dragonflies of the Sunshine Coast - Rand Rudland. This was a project of the Sunshine Coast Natural History, and Sargeant Bay Societies. Available in local bookstores.
  • Butterflies of the Sunshine Coast - Tony Greenfield and Rand Rudland. This was a project of the Sunshine Coast Natural History, and Sargeant Bay Societies.
  • Bird Checklist of the Sunshine Coast - Tony Greenfield, Rand Rudland & John Hodges. This is a project of the Sunshine Coast Natural History Society and is free for SCNHS members and can be purchased for a nominal fee for non-members.

Several additional relevant natural history guides are also available at local bookstores. These include:

  • Common Birds of Southwestern British Columbia – J. Duane Sept.
  • Common Mushrooms of the Northwest - J. Duane Sept.
  • Introducing the Dragonflies of British Columbia and the Yukon – Robert A. Cannings.
  • Mushrooms of British Columbia – Andy MacKinnon and Kem Luther.
  • Pacific Salmon Field Guide – Sean Goodwin and Martin Krkosek.
  • Plants of the Pacific Northwest Coast - Jim Pojar and Andy MacKinnon.
  • The New Beachcomber's Guide to the Pacific Northwest - J. Duane Sept.
  • Trees of the Northwest - J. Duane Sept.
